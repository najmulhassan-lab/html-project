Let me tell you about some of the benefits of mushrooms.

  1. Boosts immune system: Mushrooms contain compounds like beta-glucans that help the immune system function more efficiently.
  2. Promote gut health: Mushrooms are a source of prebiotics that feed beneficial gut bacteria, and some types may even contain probiotics that support digestion.
  3. Protects brain health: Antioxidants and B vitamins in mushrooms support brain function. Regular consumption may also lower the risk of mild cognitive impairment.
  4. Supports heart health: Some mushrooms, like shiitake, can help lower cholesterol levels.
